How to Speed Up Your Website in 2025

If your site still takes more than 3 seconds to load, you’re losing visitors—and money. Speed affects bounce rates, SEO rankings, and user trust.

Quick Wins

  • Compress and lazy-load images (use modern formats like WebP)
  • Minify CSS and JavaScript, and defer non-critical scripts
  • Use a CDN and leverage caching for static assets
  • Reduce third-party trackers and heavy fonts
